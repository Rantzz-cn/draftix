Team balancer

Add names and ranks, pick lobby size, choose how to split, then Split teams. Results open in a window you can copy to Discord. Rank scores are only used for math here — not Riot MMR.

By rank benches the lowest-rated extras when the lobby is full, then snakes starters. Random mix shuffles who plays when there are extras; if everyone fits one match, sides are fully random.
