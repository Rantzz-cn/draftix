How it works

From lobby to first round in under a minute.

  1. 01

    Open the app

    No account, no email — just visit /app and you're in.

  2. 02

    Create or join

    Host generates a 6-character code. Teammates paste it and they're connected instantly.

  3. 03

    Ban the map pool

    Captains alternate map bans until one remains — the decider.

  4. 04

    Ban agents · play

    Six agent bans (three per side) and you're locked in. Hit the lobby and play.
